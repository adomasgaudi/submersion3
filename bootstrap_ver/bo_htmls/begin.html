Everything else is under the "Quality" or "Strategy" or "Technique" section. This section explains the main goals of a language learner and the technology and strategy of achieving them. The 1st group is concerned with everything relating to motivation, philosophy, habits and all life choices that determine how how much time you will spend with the language and how focused, interested, motivated you will be.

Another way of looking at this is that, everything that lead up to you spending more time with your target language, is less directly related, while everything that you do once you're already during your dedicated language learning time is more directly related. You could think of it as trying to abtain time that will give you quantity of attention, and then once you have quantity, then what you decide to do with it determines the quality of that attention.

  • Quantity of Attention = Time dedicated
  • Quality of Attention = Efficiency / Strategy
