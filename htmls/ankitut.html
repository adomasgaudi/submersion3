The path a word takes from source to the depths of your brain.

  1. Look up

    This is the part were you've found a word that you don't know and you first want to see what it means.

  2. save

    Afterwards you want to save the word to a place where you can further edit it. This is important as it allows you to continue the next steps later, not immidiately after finding the word.

  3. import

    Next you want to take some of the words that you saved and import them into anki

  4. fix

    After you've added the words, and decided to study them, only then fix them. They might contain unnesecary parts, so we can delete that.

  5. add info

    After deleting extraneous info we might want to add info

  6. study / familiarise

    The last 2 steps were part of studying / familiarisation, but if needed you can further study the vocab in the list

    7. From here on out you start the memorisation

  7. 0 - 8mins

    In this phase you can either read additional sentences and definitions or just try to memorise

    8. pssst. btw

    if you know memory techniques you can actually bypass 0 - 8 and 8 - 1day immediately, though you won't have a good understanding of usage.

  8. 8 - 1day

    In this phase you review the word after 8 and 30 mins (my interval settings) and

  9. lvl ups
  10. 1day - mature
  11. mature review
